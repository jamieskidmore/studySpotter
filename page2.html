Typography

StudySpotter features the Ubuntu sans-serif font, which is is known for its clarity and readability, making it an ideal choice for an app that demands quick and efficient information retrieval. When users search for study spots, they need information presented in a clear and concise manner. The Ubuntu font's well-defined characters and consistent spacing enhance the legibility of text, ensuring that users can easily read and comprehend the information displayed by the app. All text is in Electric Blue #3590F3.

This is H1, 500 weight, 34pt

H1 is used for title text on the main screens including Spot Selection, Add Spot, and Favourites.

This is H2 text, 400 weight, 24pt

H2 is used to display the name of the study spot in the Spot Selection, Favourites, and Spot Details Screens. It also titles the form input areas in the Add Spot and Review Spot screens.

This is H3 text, 400 weight, 19pt

H3 is used for suheadings in the Spot Details page and in the Filter Menu.

This is H4 text, 400 weight, 17pt

H4 is used for the name of the spot reviewer in the Spot Details screen.

This is body text, 300 weight, 15pt
